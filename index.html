What We DO

A subtitle

RSJ Engineering is now and has been since its founding a technology, aeronautical and aerospace consultancy co. Our earliest engagements were as consultants to the US government in patent challenges regarding the construction patents for the hovercraft. Dr. Stanton-Jones served as an expert witness from 1987 through 1990.  Our goal, as it always has been, is to promote aeronautical and aerospace knowledge-- understanding the challenges of today for a brighter tomorrow.

Dr. Richard Stanton-Jones 

A Very Breaf History

RSJ Engineering was founded in 1989 by Dr. Richard Stanton-Jones D.Sc, FEng, M.A., M.Sc., CEng, a British aerospace engineer known at the time for his work developing the Hovercraft, aerospace production and his significant contributions to swept wing applications. In 1968, with Christopher S. Cockerell, he received the Elmer A. Sperry award for “the design, construction and application of a family of commercially useful Hovercraft.” He received an honorary doctor of Science (D.SC) in 1987 from the University of Exeter. RSJ Engineering was initially employed by the United States Department of Justice as a chief witness and subject matter expert on the construction of the hovercraft. Since its founding, RSJ Engineering has become an industry leader in aerospace consultancy and technology.
